about

story

I am a self-taught web designer that loves to design, code and create.

After working as a financial advisor for three years, I am making a career transition into web development. Even though I have a great interest in the financial markets, I have realized that developing applications gives me the energy and satisfaction that I need.

I initiated my journey as a web designer by taking a series of online courses and by putting the theory into practice through the development of a website for our padel sports team. From that point, I grew a love for coding and creating, and have taught myself to work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Adobe XD and Photoshop. I am more motivated than ever, very eager to learn and looking forward to become an expert in this field. I also have a strong interest in optimizing user experience and am digitally minded.

During my previous work experiences, I have worked in different teams as well as with various clients. This allowed me to improve as a team player and better detect the needs of a client. Over the years, I have learned that I am a critical thinker, excited to learn new things and have attention to detail. I am convinced that these skills are a strong basis for being a front-end developer.

I am currently looking for an opportunity as a web designer. In return you will receive a hard-working and enthusiastic colleague. So if you would like to work together, feel free to contact me – always happy to have a chat with some good coffee!
